# Contributing to CareCredit Tracker

## Contribution Guidelines
We are committed to building a reliable tool for managing healthcare financing. We appreciate your interest in improving this project!

## How to Contribute
1. **Fork the repository** to your own account.
2. **Create a new branch** for your feature or bug fix: `git checkout -b feature/your-feature-name`.
3. **Commit your changes** with clear, descriptive messages.
4. **Push to your fork** and open a **Pull Request** against the `main` branch.

## Reporting Issues
Please use the GitHub Issues tab. Provide a clear description of the bug, steps to reproduce it, and your environment details (browser, OS).

## Suggesting Features
We love innovation! If you have ideas to make healthcare expense tracking better, please open an issue labeled `enhancement`.

## Code of Conduct
Be respectful and professional. We follow a standard code of conduct to ensure a welcoming environment for all contributors.

## Review Process
All PRs are reviewed by the maintainers. We may suggest changes to align with the project's coding standards or architecture.
